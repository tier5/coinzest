    1. To join, send your registration details to your sponsor.
    2. All new members must provide a copy of their country official government issued color picture Identity Document during registration. ADDITIONALLY A POSITIVE PROOF OF IDENTITY PROCESS WILL BE DONE DURING REGISTRATION.
    3. A maximum of  3 accounts allowed per person. If a participant is found to have more than 3 accounts, all the accounts will be permanently blocked. The 2nd and 3rd accounts may be added as referrals of the 1st account.
    4. Each participant MAY only use one UNIQUE PHONE NUMBER, one UNIQUE EMAIL ADDRESS and one UNIQUE BTC WALLET FOR EACH AND ONLY in ONE ACCOUNT OUT OF THE MAX 3 ALLOWED PER ONE PERSON, you may not use same in ANY additional accounts.
    5. Only a maximum of 3 accounts is allowed per 1 device (1 MAC ADDRESS).
    6. IMMEDIATELY AFTER AND Upon registration, all participants must verify their accounts within three day (72 hours)
    7. A $5 penalty will be levied against the sponsor’s account if participants do not complete verification in 3 days (72 hours) from registration
    8. IF ANY ACCOUNT NOT LOGGED IN AND NO ACTIVITY FOR 30 CONSECUTIVE DAYS THE ACCOUNT WILL BE BLOCKED AND DELETED

    1. All new members must make a commitment (PH) within 7 days, failure to do so will result in a participant’s account being suspended

    3. The maximum PH for all first time accounts is $250
    4. All $100 or more PH amount request will get 20% and 80% link, 20% amount link will come anytime after 24 hours and remaining 80% amount link will come after completed and confirmed of the 20%.
    5. After fulfilling the commitment, it’s necessary to attach Screenshot of payment.
    6. There are six (6) steps to get to the maximum PH of $2000
    7. These steps cannot be skipped. Each step must be maxed each time to be allowed next higher amount maximum. Here are the steps
      1. Step one maximum : $250,
      2. Step two Maximum : $500,
      3. Step three maximum: $750,
      4. Step four maximum: $1000,
      5. Step five maximum : $1500,
      6. Step six maximum: $2000

    10. ALL NEW PH MUST BE SAME OR HIGHER THAN PREVIOUS PH TILL YOU REACH THE MAX AND RESTART THE STEPS AGAIN
    11. ALL GH REQUESTS MUST BE AFTER PH WAS REQUESTED FIRST (must be in Middle of PH to be allowed GH).
    12. Participants MUST recommit at least ONCE in 25 days. IF THERE IS NO RE-COMMITMENT ON DAY 25th THEN THE ACCOUNT WILL BE SUSPENDED

    15. Should a participant’s account remain inactive (no PH ) that account will be deleted.
